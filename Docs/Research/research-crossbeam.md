# Crossbeam — Competitor/Complement Analysis

Research compiled 2026-03-01 from YouTube video summary.

## What It Is
Built by Mike Brown (CA attorney). Uses Claude Opus subagents to parse blueprints + city correction letters, produces code-referenced action plans in ~20 minutes.

## Who It's For
Municipal permit **reviewers** — the government/supply side. Helps city staff batch-process applications and draft correction letters faster.

## How It Works
- Pipeline of Claude subagents
- Parses architectural blueprints
- Parses city correction letters
- Produces code-referenced action plans
- ~20 min processing time
- Concrete, practical, technical

## Our Positioning vs Crossbeam
| | Crossbeam | Permit Buddy |
|---|---|---|
| **User** | City permit reviewer | Homeowner/resident |
| **Side** | Supply (government) | Demand (consumer) |
| **Problem** | Too many apps to review | Don't know what to submit |
| **Solution** | Batch-process corrections | Pre-flight check before submission |
| **Value** | Faster review cycles | Fewer corrections needed |

## Why They're Complementary
- Crossbeam speeds up corrections on the city side
- Permit Buddy reduces the number of corrections needed by helping residents submit correctly the first time
- Together they attack the same bottleneck from both sides
- Could mention in pitch: "Crossbeam speeds up the review. We make sure residents submit correctly the first time."
