# Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary: San Diego Homeowners Planning a Construction Project

**Who they are:**
- Homeowners in the City of San Diego
- Planning a home improvement project (ADU, kitchen remodel, bathroom, solar panels, deck, garage conversion)
- Non-experts — they don't know building codes, zoning, or the permit process
- Likely first-time permit applicants

**Their core problem:**
- The permit process is opaque, jargon-heavy, and spread across multiple disconnected city websites
- They don't know what permits they need, what forms to submit, or what will get them rejected
- They're anxious about making expensive mistakes ($3,100+ in correction fees)
- They can't decode status codes like "PENDNG-PLANCK"

**The dominant emotion:** Fear of missing something that costs them thousands.

**What they want:** To feel prepared, competent, and like the process is workable — not to become a permit expert.

## Secondary: Small Developers / Contractors in SD

- Building multiple ADUs or doing repeat projects
- Need quick property lookups (zoning, overlays, lot data)
- Want to avoid the #1 cause of delays: incomplete submissions

## Tertiary (Hackathon Judges): City Officials / DSD Staff

- Fewer incomplete submissions = less rework for reviewers
- Makes Development Services Department look more accessible
- Complements (not replaces) existing city systems
