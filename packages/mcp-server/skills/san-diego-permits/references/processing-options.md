# San Diego Permit Processing Options

## Standard Review
- **Residential**: 4-6 weeks first review
- **Commercial**: 6-12 weeks first review
- **No-plan permits**: Same day to 1 week

## Rapid Review
- **Eligible**: Simple residential additions, kitchen/bath remodels, re-roofing, solar
- **Timeline**: Same day to 3 business days
- **Fee**: 50% surcharge on plan review fee
- **Best for**: Small residential projects needing fast turnaround

## Express Plan Check
- **Eligible**: Commercial tenant improvements, minor commercial alterations
- **Timeline**: 5-10 business days
- **Fee**: 65% surcharge on plan review fee
- **Best for**: Commercial projects with tight schedules

## Expedite Program
- **Eligible**: Large commercial projects, projects with demonstrated urgency
- **Timeline**: ~50% faster than standard
- **Fee**: Varies by project
- **Best for**: Large or time-sensitive commercial projects

## Electronic Plan Submittal (ProjectDox)
- Plans can be submitted electronically through ProjectDox
- Eliminates need for paper plan sets
- Faster corrections cycle
- Available for most permit types
