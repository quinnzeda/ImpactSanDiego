# San Diego Permit Types Reference

## Building Permit
- **When needed**: New construction, additions, structural alterations
- **Forms**: DS-345 (application), DS-560 (plan submittal checklist)
- **Timeline**: 4-12 weeks plan review
- **Code**: §129.0202

## Combination Building Permit
- **When needed**: Projects involving multiple trades (building + electrical + mechanical + plumbing)
- **Best for**: Kitchen/bathroom remodels, tenant improvements, interior renovations
- **Forms**: DS-345, DS-560
- **Timeline**: 4-8 weeks plan review

## No-Plan Residential MEP
- **When needed**: Like-for-like equipment replacement (HVAC, water heater, panel at same amperage)
- **Forms**: DS-345 only (no plans required)
- **Timeline**: Same day to 1 week
- **Note**: Most common permit type (~38K issued)

## Electrical Permit
- **When needed**: Panel upgrades, new circuits, service changes
- **Forms**: DS-345
- **Timeline**: 1-2 weeks no-plan, 4-6 weeks with plans

## Photovoltaic/Solar Permit
- **When needed**: Solar panel installations, battery storage
- **Forms**: DS-345, DS-560
- **Timeline**: 1-3 weeks (expedited available per AB 2188)
- **Subtypes**: Electrical PV Combo (~9K issued), Photovoltaic SB 379 (~5K issued)

## ADU Permit
- **When needed**: Accessory dwelling units, junior ADUs, garage conversions
- **Forms**: DS-345, DS-530 (ADU supplement), DS-560
- **Timeline**: 60 days max (state mandate)
- **See**: san-diego-adu skill for detailed ADU guidance

## Traffic Control Permit
- **When needed**: Work affecting public right-of-way (~54K issued - most common type)
- **Forms**: Traffic control plan
- **Timeline**: 1-2 weeks

## Grading Permit
- **When needed**: Earth-moving, excavation, fill, retaining walls over 3 feet
- **Forms**: DS-345, DS-164 (grading application), DS-165 (grading plan requirements)
- **Timeline**: 6-12 weeks

## Demolition Permit
- **When needed**: Full or partial structure demolition
- **Forms**: DS-345
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks
- **Special**: Asbestos survey required, utility disconnections needed

## Sign Permit
- **When needed**: Commercial signs (wall, monument, illuminated, temporary banners)
- **Forms**: DS-345
- **Timeline**: 2-6 weeks
