# San Diego Permit Exemptions (§129.0203)

Work exempt from permits (must still comply with building codes):

## Structures
- One-story detached accessory structures under 120 sq ft (sheds, playhouses)
- Prefabricated swimming pools less than 24 inches deep
- Shade cloth structures for nurseries/agriculture
- Swings and playground equipment
- Window awnings not projecting more than 54 inches
- Non-fixed partitions not over 5 feet 9 inches high

## Site Work
- Fences/walls up to 6 feet in side/rear yards
- Fences/walls up to 3 feet in front yard setback
- Retaining walls up to 3 feet (not supporting surcharge)
- Decks/platforms not more than 30 inches above grade (no basement below)

## Finish Work
- Painting and wallpapering (interior and exterior)
- Carpeting, flooring, tiling
- Countertop replacement
- Cabinet replacement (same layout)

## Equipment (like-for-like only)
- Replacing switches, receptacles, lighting fixtures
- Replacing faucets, valves, showerheads
- Replacing existing equipment with similar (same type, capacity, location)

## Important Notes
- "Exempt from permits" does NOT mean exempt from building codes
- Historic district properties may need HRB review even for exempt work
- Coastal zone properties may need Coastal Development Permit
- When in doubt, contact DSD at (619) 446-5000
